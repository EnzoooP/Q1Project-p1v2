 Description:

The whitelist bar will let you filter in tags that you like.
Example: having the <School> tag in the whitelist bar will let you only see stories that include the <School> tag in them.

The blacklist bar will let you filter out tags that you don't like.
Example: having the <School> tag in the blacklist bar will let you only see stories that doesn't include the <School> tag in them.

The random bar will direct you to stories that is picked by random.

The search bar lets you directly search for stories by typing in their title.
